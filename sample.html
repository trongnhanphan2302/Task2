Big Cities in Australia

There are 3 big cities in Australia follow the information.

Sydney

Sydney is the biggest city of Australia and also known most populous city.

Sydney, capital of New South Wales and one of Australia's largest cities,
is best known for its harbourfront Sydney Opera House,
with a distinctive sail-like design.

Melbourne

Melbourne is the second populous of Australia.

Melbourne is the capital of the Australian state of Victoria, and the second-most populous city in Australia.

Brisbane

Brisbane is the third populous city of Australia.

Brisbane, capital of Queensland, is a large city on the Brisbane River. Clustered in its South Bank cultural precinct are the Queensland Museum and Sciencentre, with noted interactive exhibitions.
